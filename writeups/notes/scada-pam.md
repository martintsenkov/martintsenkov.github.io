# Privileged Access Management in SCADA Systems

## Overview
This note presents a Privileged Access Management (PAM) policy designed for SCADA and OT environments within an energy distribution organization.

The focus is on controlling administrative access, reducing attack surface, and preventing privilege abuse in critical infrastructure.

---

## What this is
This is not a generic policy document.
It is a practical access control model tailored for real-world SCADA / OT constraints, including availability, safety, and legacy systems.

---

## Topics covered
- Privileged Access Management (PAM)
- Tiered Administration Model (Tier 0 / Tier 1 / Tier 2)
- OT jump hosts and controlled access paths
- Least privilege and Just-In-Time access
- Session monitoring and auditing
- Incident handling for compromised accounts
