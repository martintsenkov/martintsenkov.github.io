## Overview
This case study presents a Privileged Access Management (PAM) policy
designed for SCADA and OT environments within an energy distribution
organization.

The focus is on controlling administrative access, reducing attack
surface, and preventing privilege abuse in critical infrastructure.

---

## Scope and Context
The policy covers privileged access across hybrid IT/OT environments,
including SCADA servers, PLC engineering stations, HMIs, service accounts,
and remote access paths.

---

## Key Security Concepts
- Privileged Access Management (PAM)
- Tiered Administration Model (Tier 0 / 1 / 2)
- OT Jump Hosts
- Least Privilege and Just-In-Time access
- Session monitoring and auditing
- Incident response for compromised accounts
